Art museum app - effortlessly explore the museum.
ABOUT THE PROJECT
Visiting a museum in an effective and enjoyable manner can often be a challenge. At the National Gallery, the painting exhibition consists of approximately 2,300 works. Using the app, the user can easily see the shortest path to the work of art they are interested in.
Another problem is the often limited accessibility of artworks presented in museums on the Internet. This can be frustrating, especially for those who are interested in art but cannot see it in person at a museum for various reasons.
THE GOAL
The aim of the project was to create an application that would help the user efficiently navigate the museum, quickly search for information about works of art and buy a ticket.
