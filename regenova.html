Design of the Regenova Therapeutics website: User shopping experience.
CLIENT OVERVIEW
Regenova Therapeutics is a Polish company operating for 15 years, dealing with distributing medicines and medical cosmetics on the Polish market. The company cooperates with group of 2,500 specialists and has its representatives in every voivodeships. In shaping its image, Regenova Therapeutics focuses on professionalism and credibility, which is extremely important in the medical market sector. The client wanted the design to reflect these features of the company’s image.
AIMS OF THE PROJECT
1.
Create a visually appealing and minimalistic website design that aligns with the character of a company distributing medicines and medical cosmetics.
2.
Enable users to easily access information about products and the company.
3.
Create a simple shopping process that allows users to purchase products quickly and effortlessly.
4.
Create an aesthetic and easy to understand design an extensive number of company representatives.
DESIGN PROCESS
After consulting with a client and analyzing the company’s business interests and potential users’ needs, I create a sitemap for the website. The aim of this step is to construct a logical network for every step of the users' flow.
INITIAL DESIGN
The next step was creating wireframes for the Regenova Therapeutics website based on the sitemap prepared in advance. In this step, I chose a grid for the project and designed the basic layout of the website.
